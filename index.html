QUESTION 01

What is
Agrovision?

Agrovision is an AI-powered agriculture intelligence platform that uses computer vision and deep learning to monitor crops in real time. Using a smartphone camera, it identifies diseases, pests, nutrient deficiencies, and environmental stress — providing instant guidance to farmers anywhere in the world.

  • Real-time crop scanning via smartphone
  • Deep learning models trained on 2M+ plant samples
  • Multilingual chatbot interface for field workers
  • Offline-capable edge AI for remote regions
QUESTION 02

Problems
It Solves

Agrovision eliminates the dependency on expensive agronomists and slow lab testing. It democratizes agricultural intelligence, putting the power of AI diagnostics directly in the hands of smallholder farmers.

  • Eliminates late disease detection
  • Reduces pesticide overuse by 60%
  • Provides 24/7 AI agronomist support
  • Integrates satellite + ground-level data
